Weeks 1-4: Foundation Phase

  • Walking: 20-30 minutes, 4-5 days per week at a comfortable pace
  • Resistance Training: 2 sessions per week (bodyweight or light weights)
  • Focus on form and consistency, not intensity
  • Sample exercises: squats, wall push-ups, lunges, planks (10-15 reps each)
  • Rest days are important‚Äîtake 2-3 per week

Weeks 5-12: Building Phase

  • Cardio: 30-40 minutes, 5 days per week at moderate intensity
  • Mix walking with intervals of brisk walking or light jogging
  • Resistance Training: 3 sessions per week with progressive overload
  • Add weights or resistance bands, increase reps to 12-15
  • Include: dumbbell exercises, resistance band work, core strengthening
  • You should feel challenged but still able to complete workouts

Weeks 13-24: Maintenance Phase

  • Cardio: 40-50 minutes, 5-6 days per week with varied intensity
  • Include different activities: walking, swimming, cycling, dancing
  • Resistance Training: 3-4 sessions per week, full-body focus
  • Challenge yourself with heavier weights or advanced variations
  • Add one higher-intensity session per week (intervals or circuit training)
  • Listen to your body and adjust as needed

‚ö†Ô∏è Medication & Safety Notes

Mounjaro (GLP-1 medication) Management:

  • You may experience reduced appetite and occasional nausea‚Äîthis is normal
  • Prioritize protein intake even if you're not hungry (spread throughout the day)
  • Stay well-hydrated (2-3L daily) to minimize side effects
  • Eat smaller, more frequent meals if needed
  • Avoid high-fat, greasy foods which can worsen nausea
  • Take medication on the same day each week as prescribed

General Safety Guidelines:

  • Stop exercise immediately if you experience chest pain, severe dizziness, or shortness of breath
  • Stay hydrated before, during, and after exercise
  • Warm up for 5-10 minutes before exercise and cool down afterward
  • If you feel unwell, rest‚Äîconsistency over weeks matters more than individual workouts
  • Contact your healthcare provider if you have concerns about medication side effects
